About Me

For as long as I can remember, the only thing I really knew about myself was that I wanted to write. As I got a little older and people began asking me the dreaded, “So, what do you want to do with your life?”, the only answer that stayed constant was the fact that books—stories—needed to be somewhere in my future. Now, as a fourth year Professional Writing major, my goal is to tell stories that matter. My hope, more than anything else, is that my work will make my readers reflect, if only for a moment, on their lives and the world around them. When I’m not writing, I spend my time reading, sewing and designing. .
